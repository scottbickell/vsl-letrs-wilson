Voyager Sopris Learning® and Wilson College have partnered to offer the LETRS® (Language Essentials for Teachers of Reading and Spelling) course of study to Pennsylvania K–3 educators.

LETRS is proven, flexible professional development, authored by renowned literacy expert Dr. Louisa Moats, that bridges deep, meaningful research into practical classroom success. LETRS provides educators with the tools to teach language and literacy skills to K–3 or older students who struggle with reading.

Educators who complete the four graduate-level sections of LETRS:

  • Learn the science of reading instruction and understand how to identify reading disorders, such as dyslexia
  • Earn three graduate credits for each section
  • Receive a LETRS Trained Certificate of Completion
  • May transfer two sections into the Masters of Education program at Wilson College
